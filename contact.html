About E-Summit’26

E-Summit '26 is IIT Tirupati’s flagship entrepreneurship conclave, a melting pot of bold ideas, disruptive technologies, and business acumen. We bring together visionaries, industry leaders, and aspiring entrepreneurs on a single platform to foster the spirit of innovation.

Through a carefully curated lineup of keynote sessions, workshops, hackathons, and pitch competitions, we aim to bridge the gap between classroom theory and real-world execution. Whether you are a student with an idea or a startup ready to scale, E-Summit is your launchpad.
