What is E-Summit?

E-Summit ’26 is the annual flagship entrepreneurship conclave of IIT Tirupati. It is a convergence of visionary minds, industry leaders, and aspiring change-makers. The summit serves as a dynamic platform where innovation meets opportunity, designed to ignite the entrepreneurial spirit in students and professionals alike.

Our Objective

Our core objective is to Architect Impact. We aim to bridge the gap between ideation and execution by providing a robust ecosystem of mentorship, funding, and networking. E-Summit strives to cultivate a culture where disruptive ideas are nurtured into sustainable ventures that solve real-world problems.

Why Attend E-Summit?

Network

Connect with a diverse community of over 500+ attendees, including venture capitalists, angel investors, successful founders, and like-minded peers.

Learn

Gain actionable insights through masterclasses, fireside chats, and workshops led by industry pioneers who have scaled from zero to one.

Transform

Validate your startup ideas, find potential co-founders, and pitch to investors ready to fund the next big thing.

What You’ll Experience

  • Keynote Sessions from industry leaders
  • Startup Expo showcasing innovation
  • Pitch Competitions with curated jury feedback
  • Speaker sessions on market trends
  • High-impact Networking with founders & peers

Who Is It For?

Student Entrepreneurs Early-stage Startups Investors & VCs Tech Enthusiasts Corporate Innovators Researchers
